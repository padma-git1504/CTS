Our Community Activities

Our local community is committed to building a stronger and more vibrant neighborhood. This video highlights recent events including our Clean-Up Drive, Tree Plantation Campaign, and the Cultural Fest held last month. Residents of all ages came together to promote environmental sustainability, celebrate diversity, and enjoy meaningful social interaction. The clean-up drive helped remove over 500 kg of waste from our local parks and streets. The plantation initiative saw the addition of 200+ new trees in public areas, while the cultural evening featured performances by local artists, traditional food stalls, and engaging games for children and adults alike. We encourage everyone to be part of our next event! Your participation strengthens the sense of community and makes our neighborhood a better place to live.
